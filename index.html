About VRMS

Voter Registration Management System (VRMS) is a secure digital platform designed to streamline voter registration, verification, approval, and E-EPIC generation.

The system enables citizens to apply for voter registration online, upload required documents, track application status, and download their Electronic EPIC (E-EPIC) after approval. VRMS ensures transparency, accuracy, and efficiency in the voter onboarding process through secure authentication and administrative verification workflows.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get approval?

Typically, verification takes 7-15 days depending on your constituency and document clarity.

Can I register if I live overseas?

Yes, NRIs can register using Form 6A. You will need your passport details.
